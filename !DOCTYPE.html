❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is TrustMate free to join?
Yes — registration is completely free for both vehicle owners and drivers.
How long does driver verification take?
Our compliance team typically reviews applications within 1-2 business days. You will receive an email confirmation once approved.
What documents does a driver need to register?
SA ID copy, valid driver's licence, headshot photo, and screenshots of your Uber/Bolt/inDrive profile showing your rating and trip count. An Adhuru criminal check is optional but recommended.
Can an owner list more than one vehicle?
Yes — owners can list an unlimited number of vehicles. Each listing is reviewed and approved separately.
Is my personal information safe?
Absolutely. Contact details and documents are only shared with matched parties. We are fully POPIA compliant.
